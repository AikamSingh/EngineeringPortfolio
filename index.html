About

Engineering is how I think.

I'm a Computer Engineering student at the University of Michigan focused on embedded systems and hardware design. I'm drawn to work where the software has to talk directly to the physical world — where a bug doesn't just crash an app, it can affect a vehicle moving at highway speed.

I've been building things since high school — racing solar cars, designing PCBs, and writing firmware. In 2026 I drove the University of Michigan solar car cross-country from Michigan to California as part of the race crew, maintaining electrical systems on the road every day.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy working on projects that combine hardware and software in ways that are useful or just interesting. I'm also interested in the intersection of embedded systems and machine learning.
